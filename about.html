About The Site Owner

Hello! I'm Nameless (they/it), the creator and maintainer of this website, as well as a number enthusiast and hobbyist programmer. I've been a passionate fan of Shuuen no Shiori since February 11th, 2019, and own an 80+ member public Shuuen no Shiori Discord server! My favorite character is E-ki, and my favorite club is the film club.

Back in November 2019, this guide was originally a carrd (now deprecated) created to serve as an up-to-date guide / resource for people looking to get into Shuuen no Shiori in the present day (since, y'know, the fanbase isn't the most... alive), but was recreated as its own separate webpage late March of 2023! I'm usually more than happy to talk to people about Shuuen, so don't be afraid to come talk to me about it!
