Albums / Songs

Audio Files (FLAC)

There are 5 albums in Shuuen no Shiori. There is also an exclusive CD / album with two songs translated here (Shuuen Scanlations). The two songs are included in Shuuen -Re:mind- as well.

Re:Houkago -Shuuen Game- (AmeSubs) (August 11th, 2012)
A doujin CD that acted sort of like a one-shot album for Shuuen. Included the original IA versions of the first four songs (except the NND / "final" version of D-ne's song) and 2 new songs.

(YT) (Spotify) Shuuen -Re:write- (Shuuen Scanlations) (March 13th, 2013)
First major album of Shuuen! Focused on the occult club. Included new unique vocaloid covers of the first cast's songs plus Underdog Supremacist (Common Heroes and Bookmark of Demise were left mostly unchanged). Included 4 new songs and a piano arrangement mini-album in the limited edition.

Unlucky -Makki- @ School Trip (eiyakutachi) (November 24th, 2013)
Spinoff / "Summer AU" album. Included 7 new songs sung by IA.

(YT) (Spotify) Shuuen -Re:mind- (March 25th, 2015)
Second major album of Shuuen! Focused on the film club. Included 13 new tracks and the 5 character songs of the second cast. Only one song received an official PV.

(YT) Shuuen -Re:act- (September 23rd, 2015)
Third major album of Shuuen! A special album which included the entire cast's character songs covered by their respective voice actors. Included 2 new songs. In its limited editions, the album also came with a CD Drama with 8 tracks and two special tracks.
