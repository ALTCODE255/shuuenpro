A Guide to Shuuen no Shiori

by @ALTCODE255

Introduction

The Bookmark of Demise Project (終焉ノ栞プロジェクト, Shuuen no Shiori Purojekuto) (May 12th, 2012 – January 27th, 2016), colloquially known as Shuuenpro, is a multimedia Vocaloid series created by:

  • 150P (music)
  • Suzumu (スズム) (story & lyrics)
  • komine (こみね) (illustration)
  • Saine (さいね) (illustration & character design)

This website is meant to serve as an actively maintained resource for both existing and new fans of the series. It contains up-to-date translation masterlists on Shuuenpro's:

  • Albums / Songs
  • Light Novels
  • Manga
  • Album Crossfades & Song MVs

If you're a new fan looking to get into Shuuen no Shiori, click here to proceed to the New Fan's Guide.
