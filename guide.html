Guide

  1. Songs (EP 1 - 4)
  2. Manga (Chapter 1 to Chapter 11.4)
    OR
    Light Novel 1
  3. Light Novel 2 (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED - Manga skips much of this novel)
  4. Songs (EP ***s + 0)
  5. (OPTIONAL) Shuuen -Re:write- - Translations
  6. Manga (Chapter 12 to Chapter 33)
    OR
    Light Novels 3 & 4 (Not recommended due to lack of translations - see here for more)
  7. (OPTIONAL) Shuuen -Re:mind- - Translations (Contains Film Club songs)
  8. (OPTIONAL) Listen to the drama CD! and/or view the various albums Shuuen has and their crossfades!

If you're thirsty for more Shuuenpro, check out the links under the 'Masterlists' dropdown and/or join our unofficial Discord server!
